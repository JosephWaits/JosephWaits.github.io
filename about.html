About Me

Hello, my name is Joseph. Originally, I'm from South Carolina, but I've lived in Newport since 2007,
no matter what the University of Rhode Island tells you. I entered the semester as a Film major, but as the
existential dread of possibly graduating with out-of-state debt and a dubious Film degree hit me, I quickly looked
towards Computer Science.

But before I began this college mumbo jumbo, I had the privelege to sail all around the world, from South America to Antarctica,
and all points in between. I've trampled under foot all seven continents, but I believe my favorite memory was taking
the train across Russia. You should check it out.
